FAQ:

IELTS is the International English Language Testing System, which measures the language proficiency of people who want to study or work where English is used as a language of communication.

You can register online on the official IELTS website or through an authorized test center.

IELTS consists of four sections: Listening, Writing, Reading, Speaking.

The highest score you can get in IELTS is 9.0.
